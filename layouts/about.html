What we're all about

Our story began right here in Montreal in 1998 with the release of the world’s first pay-for-play digital jukebox. Since then, we have transformed into a multi-application platform offering a variety of engaging and interactive experiences. TouchTunes is one of only a few platforms able to deliver end-to-end in-venue interactive entertainment solutions, encompassing hardware, software and network operations.
